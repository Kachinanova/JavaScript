2018 Bucket List

It's time to get out and explore Arizona! I've lived here my entire life and still haven't seen most of what this state has to offer. (This list is a work in progress. Items will be added as new events open and/or are found.)



Grand Canyon Skywalk

Step out onto the Skywalk at Eagle Point for a once in a life time view of the canyon.


Painted Desert and Pretrified Forest

Explore the vibrant rock display and learn about the old forest.


Out of Africa

Take a safari jeep tour in Arizona's Out of Africa Park.
